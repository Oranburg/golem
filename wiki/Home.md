# Law of the Golem

A research project on AI, agency, and responsibility for the things we set in motion. An artificial system is a golem in an old and exact sense: a thing a person animates that acts in the world and cannot answer for what it does. The project asks when an automated lethal or irreversible decision is forbidden and when it is permitted, and argues that the question that sorts the cases is older than the technology: when a thing that cannot answer causes harm, is there still a person the law can reach?
