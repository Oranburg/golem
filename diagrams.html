Diagrams

The argument, the nested golem, and the Jewish-law structure, drawn.

The argument in one path

flowchart TD
  A["An automated system causes a lethal or irreversible harm"] --> B{"Did a human make the discriminating call,
or only set the machine in motion?"}
  B -->|"a human made the call
(CyberKnife, supervised drone)"| C["Liability runs to the human.
The fire is his arrow."]
  B -->|"the machine made the call"| D{"Is there a responsible human
the law can still reach?"}
  D -->|"yes: operator, commander, founder"| E["Run it back to him.
Veil-piercing reaches the human behind the golem."]
  D -->|"no: full autonomy, AI-run firm, DAO"| F["The accountability gap.
No defendant; the residue the court cannot reach."]

The trigger words do not sort the cases; the question that does is whether an answerable human survives.

The nested golem: the corporate decision to kill

flowchart LR
  S["Sovereign / State"] --> P["Private military corporation
(a corporate golem)"]
  P --> AI["AI targeting system
(a weapon golem)"]
  AI --> K["The act: a strike"]
  S -. "delegates authority" .-> P
  P -. "delegates the decision" .-> AI
  K -. "who answers?" .-> Q{"At each layer, is there
an answerable human?"}

A state animates a corporation that animates a machine. The decision to kill sits three non-answering layers deep.

The Jewish-law chain

flowchart TD
  G["A thing acts and cannot answer
(Sanhedrin 65b: the golem returns to dust)"] --> H{"Is the intermediary a competent person
who could refuse?"}
  H -->|"yes (a piqqeach)"| I["No agency for a wrong.
The doer bears it; the sender steps back."]
  H -->|"no (a golem, an AI: lav bar chiyuva)"| J["The whole act reverts to the sender.
The fire is his arrow; the keeper is forewarned."]
  J --> R{"Is the act the taking of a life?"}
  R -->|"yes"| K["Directness is required; and a residue remains:
exempt in the human court, liable before Heaven."]
  R -->|"no"| L["Liability runs to the sender in full."]

Agency, the fire that is the sender's arrow, the directness the taking of a life demands, and the residue the court cannot reach.
